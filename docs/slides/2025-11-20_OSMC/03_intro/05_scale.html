At scale, the number of loggers can grow exponentially:

      architecture-beta
        group server1(server)[Server 1]
        group server2(server)[Server 2]
        group central(server)[Centralized Logging]

        service app1(logos:docker-icon)[App 1] in server1
        service app2(logos:docker-icon)[App 2] in server1
        service app3(logos:docker-icon)[App 3] in server1
        service syslog-srv1(database)[Syslog] in server1

        service app4(logos:docker-icon)[App 5] in server2
        service app5(logos:docker-icon)[App 6] in server2
        service app6(logos:docker-icon)[App 7] in server2
        service syslog-srv2(database)[Syslog] in server2

        service log-collector(database)[Log Collector] in central

        app1:T -- B:syslog-srv1
        app2:L -- R:syslog-srv1
        app3:B -- T:syslog-srv1

        app4:T -- B:syslog-srv2
        app5:R -- L:syslog-srv2
        app6:B -- T:syslog-srv2

        syslog-srv1:L -- R:log-collector
        syslog-srv2:R -- L:log-collector
