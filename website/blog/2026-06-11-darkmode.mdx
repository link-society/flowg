:tada:
**[FlowG v0.59.0](https://github.com/link-society/flowg/releases/tag/v0.59.0)**
has been released, and it includes (since **v0.58.0**) a dark mode for the
frontend!

<!-- truncate -->

## Frontend Redesign

As stated [a couple months ago](/blog/road-to-stable#frontend-redesign), one of
the goals for the future **1.0.0** release is a complete redesign of the
frontend, including:

 - removal of *Tailwind CSS*, and fully committing to *React MUI*
 - reorganization of the codebase to follow best practices
 - internationalization

This endeavor has progressed tremendously. And in this new release, we are
closing a feature request that was asked 1 year ago: **Dark Mode support**.
